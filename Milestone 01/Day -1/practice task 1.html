My Travel Blog

In the beginning I didn’t keep any records about my travels. Even when changing laptops, I didn’t transfer old files with plans, maps, and descriptions of places worth visiting. I must admit, I really regret it now. Later, when I wanted to talk about some particularly interesting place, I began to realize that details were falling out of my memory. I remember that I ate in a very authentic restaurant in a village not far from Asti, but the name of the village has already been erased from my memory. And I began to describe my travels very briefly in one of the social networks. First, a week-long trip with one or two posts. And lately I’ve been keeping a diary in which I record the most vivid impressions every day.
